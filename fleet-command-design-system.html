Brand & Logo

The Fleet Command OS logo is a four-petal pinwheel. The top-left petal uses Signal Green (#72E3AD) as the brand accent; the remaining three petals use Deep Navy (#0F182A) on light backgrounds, and shift to Steel Blue (#A3B8D0) on dark backgrounds. Always maintain clear space equal to the logo width around it.

Logo Rules
Do
Use the SVG mark at approved sizes (16/24/32/48/64px). Maintain clear space. Flip dark petals to white on dark backgrounds. Pair with "Fleet Command" in Space Grotesk 700.
Don't
Don't rotate the logo. Don't recolor the green petal. Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast overlay. Don't stretch or distort the aspect ratio.

Do's & Don'ts

Hard rules that protect the integrity of the visual language. Consistency across all product surfaces is non-negotiable.

✓ Do
  • Use color purposefully — every status color has clear semantic meaning
  • Keep KPI numbers large and prominent — they are the hero of every card
  • Show trends alongside every metric (up/down arrow + % change)
  • Use real functional icons for navigation, not decorative illustrations
  • Keep sidebar collapsed by default on narrow/tablet screens
  • Use skeleton loaders while data fetches — never show blank space
✕ Don't
  • Don't use more than 3 chart types on a single dashboard view
  • Don't use red for anything other than critical/danger states
  • Don't place text directly over map layers without a backdrop card
  • Don't animate every element — reserve motion for key interactions only
  • Don't use more than 2 font weights on a single widget
  • Don't use decorative gradients on data — backgrounds only
